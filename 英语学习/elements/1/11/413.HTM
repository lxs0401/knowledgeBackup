adv. 向外地; 外表地

分布：向外(87%)，在外(6%)，外表上(5%)，=outward.(2%)
US [-wɚdz/ (mainly US outward) 
adverb 
■going or pointing away from a particular place or towards the outside 向外，朝外
•The door opens outwards.这扇门朝外开。
•It's much healthier to direct your emotions outwards than to bottle them up inside you.把你的情绪发泄出来，比闷在心里更有益于健康。
Opposite inwards
