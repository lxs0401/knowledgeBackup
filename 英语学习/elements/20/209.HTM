inspection

[ɪnˈspekʃn] (四六托雅宝 3588/3194)
n. 检查; 检验; 视察; 检阅

分布：检查(84%)，视察(16%)
→来自inspect,检查，巡视。

视察;认真看(inspect+ion)[spect,spic=look,see,表示"看"]

【记】inspect（vt 检查；视察）根：spect看
【参】retrospect（n 回顾）；introspect（v 反省）
noun countable or uncountable 
 IMPROVER  
■when you look at something carefully, or an official visit to a building or organization to check that everything is correct and legal 检查；视察
•Her passport seemed legitimate, but on closer inspection, it was found to have been altered.她的护照看似合法，但仔细检查，发现做过手脚。
•She arrived to carry out/make a health and safety inspection of the building.她来对大楼进行安全卫生检查。
